Producing Excellence in Asia

With our 12 year history in movie production, in multiple languages and markets, under various banners, we have gained invaluable experience. RAMA Reels and associated banners have been involved in over 50 productions in India. With this foundation of success, RAMA is now reaching out to the rest of Asia with a vision to unite Asia under a singular Entertainment umbrella.

By showcasing the unique talents of the business and entertainment worlds in Asia, we will bring long overdue and highly deserved recognition to, not only the Asian Entertainment industry, but the entire region of Asia. RAMA United Asia Entertainment

Good storytelling is a universal art, not dependent on language or culture. At RAMA, we desire to help break down the cultural divides and to unify all of us though our common love of entertainment. Great entertainment can make people laugh, cry, or scream in any language. Therefore, we focus on bringing you the best of what Asia has to offer. "As we look toward unifying Asia into a cohesive entertainment market, we will continue to grow and expand our presence in the area and welcome partners who value our vision"

  • China Partnership

    RAMA China has acquired a stake in and signed a Strategic Cooperation Agreement with Uing Holdings of Shanghai, China. Uing Holdings consists of Uing Media, Uing Education, and Uing Distribution. Together, we will focus on project investments and financing, global film and TV copyright transactions and combine our efforts to initiate the Asian Film Awards Project.

  • Belt and Road Initiative

    Promoting ‘Belt and Road Initiative’ RAMA China is now opening office in Ningbo China to do a series of movie projects based out of Ningbo. These projects will showcase multiple cultures and will be featured in markets across Asia. Culture Ministry of Haishu District in Ningbo has agreed to support the project by all means and the pre production work is now in progress.
